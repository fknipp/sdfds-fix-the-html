My biggest dreams

When I was young, I had a lots of dreams. Some of these were:

      * Celebrating parties with thousands of friends
      * Visiting all countries around the globe
      * Riding the fastest cars on earth

But as time went by, all these dreams were lost. Someday, I've met a person, who gave me a good advice:

Dreams are necessary to life.

I want to continue to life a fulfilled life. So, I started to make a list, what I want to do with whom and when. 

What?                     With whom?       When?
=====                     ==========       =====
Hike in a lonely valley   My partner       Next year
Play an instrument        Alone            Next month
Drink a cool beer         My best friend   Now
About me

I am in search of my dreams. **The bigger, the better.**
